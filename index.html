About Me

Hi there! I'm Yiqing (Simon) Zhi, a passionate mechanical engineering student at the University of Waterloo with a love for creating innovative and functional designs. I specialize in CAD modeling, engineering analysis, and enjoy turning complex problems into simple, elegant solutions.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring new engineering technologies, working on personal projects, or collaborating with teams on challenging mechanical systems. I also enjoy traveling around the world with my friends and parents, and playing sports such as snowboading, basketball, badminton, and cheering passionately for the Toronto Blue Jays - Go Jays Go!
