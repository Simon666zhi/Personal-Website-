🎯 Project Overview

The Four-Foot Spider Robot project focused on creating a detailed 3D CAD model of a quadruped robot inspired by biomimetic design principles. I developed the complete mechanical structure in SolidWorks, designing each component with precision and documenting the assembly through technical drawings.

At its core, this design features a sophisticated 12 degree-of-freedom (DOF) system, with each of the four legs incorporating three articulated joints. This kinematic configuration would enable complex gaits including walking, turning, and adaptive terrain navigation if physically implemented. The mechanical design draws inspiration from arachnid biomechanics, with careful consideration for stability, weight distribution, and structural efficiency in the CAD model.

⚡ My Contributions

🔧 Mechanical Design & CAD Development

  • CAD Modeling: Complete 3D model development in SolidWorks, including assembly design, interference checking, and bill of materials generation for manufacturability assessment
  • Kinematic Analysis: Designed and validated the 12 DOF kinematic chain with proper joint ranges of motion and workspace analysis for each leg assembly
  • Component Design: Developed detailed part models for body chassis, leg segments, and joint mechanisms with proper mounting features and structural reinforcement
  • Iterative Refinement: Multiple design iterations optimizing for weight reduction, structural efficiency, and improved kinematics through motion studies and design reviews

📐 Technical Documentation

  • Engineering Drawings: Created comprehensive technical drawings with full dimensions, tolerances, and GD&T specifications for all major components
  • Assembly Documentation: Developed exploded views, assembly sequences, and part relationship documentation for manufacturing guidance
  • Design Specifications: Documented material selections, design rationale, and performance requirements for the mechanical system
  • BOM Generation: Compiled detailed bill of materials with part numbers, quantities, and specifications for potential fabrication

🔬 Design Analysis & Validation

  • Motion Studies: Performed SolidWorks motion analysis to validate kinematic configurations and identify potential collision issues
  • Interference Checking: Systematic verification of clearances between components throughout the full range of motion
  • Design for Manufacturing: Evaluated manufacturability of components considering 3D printing constraints, assembly accessibility, and tolerance stackup
  • Weight Analysis: Calculated mass properties and center of gravity positions to assess stability characteristics of the design

🎯 Project Planning & Execution

  • Design Workflow: Established systematic CAD workflow from concept sketches through detailed component design to final assembly
  • Version Control: Maintained organized file structure and design revision tracking throughout the project development
  • Design Reviews: Conducted self-assessments and design validation checks at key milestones to ensure quality and completeness
  • Portfolio Development: Created visualization materials including renderings, animations, and this interactive web presentation to showcase the design
